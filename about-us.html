About Central High

Central High School, previously Central Collegiate Academy, and originally named Central High School is the oldest public secondary school in Detroit, Michigan; it is owned by the Detroit Public Schools.

As Detroit's oldest high school,Central values education and prepares students for their future. Central's 5 core values are excellence, intergrity, harmony, creativity, and empathy. at central we strive for greatness. Central has also enjoyed a tradition of athletic success.Central High School dominated city league men's basketball during the early twentieth century, winning championship titles in 1906, 1907 and 1909.
